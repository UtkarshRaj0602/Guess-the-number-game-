Instructions
  • A random number is generated at the start of each round, which is in between the number's 1 and 20.
  • Your task is to guess that random number and for that a total of 8 life's has been provided to you.
  • If you guess the random number in the first go, then your high score will be added to 100 points with a decrease of 20 points for every 2 life's.
  • The more life's you take to guess the number, the more high score you loose by 10 points.
